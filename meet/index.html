Marty Schmidt

Marty is primarily a studying self-taught pianist since his mid-teens, and composing music all along, theatre is what ultimately brought him to the stage. Performing his own music in original projects, all while refining his unusual 'guitar on keys' sound, where he is comfortable with everything from Elton John to Black Sabbath. Gravity Shift is a perfect vehicle to showcase the keys, vocals, and "guitar", in a band he calls "the most fun band I've ever been in!" We have hereby dubbed him: the Mad Hatter.
